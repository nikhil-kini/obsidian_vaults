* **Keep it under 2 min**
* **Should contain:**
	* Present Portion
		* Current role
		* Success metric
		* Recent Achievement
	* Past Portion
		* Highlight Key strength
			* Identify 2-3 attribute
			* Match your experience to the attributes
			* Select 1 key moment
	* Future Portion
		* What makes you good for the role



Note:
- **Break the ice with "How are you feeling today?" to get them in the movement.**
- **End with "When will the feedback be out, with HR to followup?", "What questions could i have asked?"**
